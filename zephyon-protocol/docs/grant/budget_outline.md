# ZephiPay — Budget Outline

## Overview

Grant funding would primarily support continued protocol refinement, frontend usability improvements, infrastructure hardening, development tooling, public MVP preparation, and ecosystem-facing documentation.

The current project has already progressed into a functioning Solana devnet prototype with frontend integration, deterministic receipt infrastructure, and operational payment execution. Funding would accelerate continued refinement, reliability expansion, and broader ecosystem accessibility.

---

## Development Infrastructure

### Purpose
Support continued protocol development, frontend iteration, local testing environments, Solana tooling workflows, and infrastructure scalability preparation.

### Allocation Areas
- High-performance development workstation
- Local development environment support
- Protocol tooling and infrastructure utilities
- Frontend development tooling
- Testing and debugging infrastructure

### Rationale
Modern Solana development workflows, frontend infrastructure, stress testing, and future audit preparation require reliable high-performance development infrastructure to maintain rapid iteration and operational stability.

## Budget Philosophy

The project prioritizes lean operational execution, infrastructure reliability, usability-focused development, and responsible ecosystem contribution.

Funding is intended to accelerate refinement, operational maturity, and broader accessibility to blockchain-powered payment infrastructure rather than support speculative ecosystem expansion.
