# Zephyon Trust Score (ZTS)

## Status

Working Draft

June 2026

---

# Purpose

The Zephyon Trust Score (ZTS) is a reputation framework designed to measure trustworthiness through verifiable economic activity.

Unlike traditional review systems that rely primarily on subjective ratings or promotional visibility, ZTS is grounded in actual participation within the Zephyon ecosystem.

Trust is established through transactions, receipts, settlement history, verification systems, and long-term ecosystem engagement.

The objective of ZTS is not simply to measure trust.

The objective is to transform trust into a portable and measurable asset that benefits participants who consistently contribute value to the ecosystem.

---

# Trust Philosophy

The Zephyon Trust Score is built upon a simple principle:

Trust should be earned through activity rather than purchased through capital.

Within the Zephyon ecosystem, trust emerges from participation, reliability, and verifiable economic behavior.

The framework is intentionally designed to reward long-term contributors while discouraging manipulation, abuse, and purely speculative activity.

Trust should become stronger as participants demonstrate consistent reliability over time.

---

# Core Principles

ZTS is designed around five foundational principles.

## Trust Must Be Earned

Trust is established through real economic activity rather than purchased through advertising, promotion, token ownership, or financial influence.

Participants earn trust through actions rather than claims.

---

## Trust Must Be Verifiable

Trust should be supported by objective evidence.

Examples include:

* Verified Receipts
* Settlement History
* Transaction Records
* Merchant Verification
* Verified Ecosystem Activity

Trust should be measurable rather than assumed.

---

## Trust Must Be Portable

Reputation should remain attached to participants and continue developing over time.

A participant's trust history should not disappear simply because transaction volume fluctuates.

Trust should compound through consistent participation.

---

## Trust Must Be Transparent

Participants should understand how trust is earned, maintained, and potentially reduced.

The framework should remain understandable and predictable.

---

## Trust Must Provide Value

Trust should unlock meaningful advantages throughout the ecosystem.

The purpose of ZTS is not merely to generate a score.

The purpose is to create value through reputation.

---

# What Increases ZTS

The following activities may positively contribute to ZTS.

## Successful Transactions

Completed transactions demonstrate reliability and ecosystem participation.

---

## Verified Receipts

Receipts provide verifiable proof of economic activity.

As receipt history grows, confidence in participant reliability may increase.

---

## Account Longevity

Long-term participation demonstrates consistency and commitment.

Trust should strengthen through time and continued activity.

---

## Settlement Reliability

Participants with consistent settlement performance may receive additional trust benefits.

Reliability is a fundamental component of trust.

---

## Merchant Verification

Verified businesses may receive additional trust recognition.

Verification strengthens confidence in participant legitimacy.

---

## Ecosystem Participation

Constructive and consistent participation within the ecosystem may contribute positively to trust development.

---

Exact weighting mechanisms remain under development.

---

# What Does Not Increase ZTS

The following activities do not directly increase trust.

* Purchasing ZERA
* Staking ZERA
* Advertising Spend
* Referral Purchases
* Promotional Campaigns
* Speculative Activity

Trust cannot be purchased.

Trust must be earned through participation and verifiable activity.

---

# What Decreases ZTS

The following activities may negatively impact trust.

## Failed Settlements

Repeated settlement failures may reduce confidence and reliability.

---

## Fraudulent Activity

Verified fraud, malicious behavior, or abuse may result in significant trust penalties.

---

## Chargebacks and Disputes

Excessive unresolved disputes may negatively impact participant reputation.

---

## Ecosystem Abuse

Spam, manipulation, exploit attempts, or platform abuse may reduce trustworthiness.

---

## Account Enforcement Actions

Restrictions, suspensions, or severe violations may negatively affect trust standing.

---

Exact penalty mechanisms remain under development.

# Trust Categories

The Zephyon Trust Score framework is intended to support multiple participant types.

While each category may utilize different weighting mechanisms, all trust scores remain grounded in verifiable economic activity.

## Merchant Trust Score

Measures merchant reputation, settlement history, verification status, and commercial activity.

---

## User Trust Score

Measures long-term participation, transaction history, and ecosystem engagement.

---

## Business Trust Score

Measures organizational reliability, transaction success, and commercial reputation.

---

## Agent Trust Score

Measures machine-to-machine reliability, settlement history, service performance, and verified agent activity.

# Long-Term Vision

The long-term objective of ZTS is to establish a portable trust layer for commerce throughout the Zephyon ecosystem.

As the ecosystem expands, trust may become increasingly important for:

* Customers evaluating merchants
* Businesses evaluating counterparties
* Developers integrating ecosystem participants
* Agents selecting services and providers

Over time, ZTS may evolve into a unified trust framework supporting human, business, and agent commerce while remaining grounded in verifiable economic activity.

Trust should be earned.

Trust should be measurable.

Trust should be portable.

Trust should be beneficial.

Most importantly, trust should reflect real economic behavior rather than financial influence.
