## Status

Working Draft

# Purpose

The Subscription-or-Stake model is designed to provide flexible access to premium Zephyon services while creating sustainable utility for ZERA.

Participants may choose between:

* Paying a recurring subscription fee
* Acquiring and staking ZERA

Both options provide access to premium ecosystem capabilities.

This approach allows participants to engage with Zephyon regardless of their familiarity with digital assets while creating a clear path toward deeper ecosystem participation.

The objective is not to force token ownership.

The objective is to create optional participation pathways.

---

# Core Philosophy

Zephyon is designed to remain accessible to everyone.

Individuals, merchants, businesses, and developers should be capable of using the ecosystem without acquiring ZERA.

Premium services should remain available through traditional subscription models.

ZERA exists to provide an alternative path.

Participants may choose to:

Pay Monthly

OR

Stake ZERA

Both paths unlock similar capabilities while supporting different participant preferences.

---

# Why Subscription or Stake Exists

Traditional software platforms generally operate through recurring subscription fees.

Crypto ecosystems often require mandatory token ownership.

Zephyon seeks to combine the strengths of both models.

Subscription access provides simplicity.

Stake access provides ownership and alignment.

This structure allows participants to choose whether they prefer recurring operational costs or long-term ecosystem participation.

# Stake Access Model

The Stake Access Model is designed to align participants with long-term ecosystem growth.

Participants may stake ZERA to unlock premium capabilities without requiring recurring subscription payments.

The objective is to transform ZERA from a passive asset into a productive ecosystem asset.

---

## Stake Maintenance

Stake-based access requires active participation.

If the required stake is removed:

* Premium access may be reduced
* Certain capabilities may become unavailable
* Grace periods may apply

Specific implementation details remain under development.

The guiding principle is simple:

Stake Maintains Access.
